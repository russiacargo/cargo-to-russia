What to Include in Your Quote Request

To get the most accurate quote, please share the following details when you contact us:

1

Cargo Type

What are you shipping? (commercial goods, personal items, documents, vehicle)

2

Weight & Dimensions

Total weight in kg and package dimensions (L × W × H)

3

Pickup Location

Your address in Dubai or anywhere in the UAE

4

Destination in Russia

City and full delivery address (Moscow, St. Petersburg, etc.)

5

Service Speed

Express (1–3 days), standard (5–10 days), or economy (sea freight)

6

Timeline

When do you need the cargo to arrive?
