Major Russian Ports We Serve

Our shipping network connects Dubai to all major Russian ports, providing comprehensive cargo shipping to Russia through strategic maritime routes.

St. Petersburg

Russia's largest port and main gateway to the Baltic Sea. We offer regular container shipping to Russia through this strategic port.

Transit time: 25-30 days from Dubai

Novorossiysk

Russia's main port on the Black Sea and a key hub for Russian shipping lines. Ideal for cargo to southern Russia.

Transit time: 18-22 days from Dubai

Vladivostok

Russia's largest Pacific port and gateway to the Far East. Strategic for shipping to eastern Russia.

Transit time: 35-40 days from Dubai

Kaliningrad

Russia's westernmost port and an ice-free Baltic port. Important for Russian container shipping lines to Europe.

Transit time: 28-32 days from Dubai

Murmansk

Russia's northernmost ice-free port. Strategic for shipping to Russia's Arctic regions and northern territories.

Transit time: 30-35 days from Dubai

Rostov-on-Don

Important river port connecting to the Black Sea. Key for shipping to Russia's southern regions.

Transit time: 20-25 days from Dubai
