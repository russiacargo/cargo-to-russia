Courier Service from Dubai to Russia: Send Parcels with Confidence

Everything you need to know about sending parcels, documents, and small packages from Dubai to Russia with reliable courier and delivery services.

Since major global couriers like DHL, FedEx, and UPS suspended direct services to Russia, sending a parcel from Dubai to Russia requires specialized logistics providers who have built alternative shipping routes through third-country hubs.

D2R Cargo operates established courier routes from Dubai to all major Russian cities, including Moscow, St. Petersburg, Novorossiysk, Kazan, and Yekaterinburg, with door-to-door tracking and customs clearance included.

What Can You Send by Courier to Russia?

Our courier service from Dubai to Russia handles a wide range of items:

  • Documents — business contracts, legal papers, certificates
  • Personal parcels — gifts, clothing, personal belongings
  • E-commerce orders — electronics, fashion, cosmetics from Dubai
  • Business samples — product samples, prototypes, spare parts
  • Small commercial cargo — up to 50 kg per shipment

Prohibited items include hazardous materials, weapons, counterfeit goods, and items restricted by Russian customs regulations. Contact us if you are unsure about your item.

How Much Does It Cost to Send a Parcel to Russia?

Courier costs from Dubai to Russia are based on weight, dimensions, and service speed:

  • Documents (under 0.5 kg): From $25–35
  • Small parcels (1–5 kg): From $45–75
  • Medium parcels (5–20 kg): From $8–12 per kg
  • Larger parcels (20–50 kg): From $6–10 per kg

All prices include pickup from your location in Dubai, customs documentation, and delivery to the recipient's address in Russia. Insurance is available as an add-on.

Required Documents for Courier Shipments

To send a parcel from Dubai to Russia, you will need:

  1. Sender's full name, address, and phone number
  2. Receiver's full name, address, and phone number in Russia
  3. Description of contents and declared value
  4. Copy of sender's Emirates ID or passport

For commercial shipments, a commercial invoice is also required. D2R Cargo can help you prepare all documentation as part of our service.
